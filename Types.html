types of computer:

  • software.

tablets

A portable computer integrated into a multitouch-sensitive screen that use on-screen keyboard (you can connect an external keyboard)

laptop

A Laptop computer is a portable computer that has a keyboard, monitor and other devices integrated into a single compact case

Stationary computers

A desktop computer is intended for use at a single location.

Mainframe

large, expensive computers that supports many users simultaneously , Used in business that manage large amounts of data.

Supercomputer

Is especially deigned computer that can perform complex calculations extremely rapidly.

Embedded computers

is a specially designed computer chip that resides in another device, such as your car, a drone or the electronic thermostat in your home. .
